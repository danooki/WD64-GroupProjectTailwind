NORTHRACE MOUNTAIN BIKES

-Engineered for the Wild- Handmade Precision Bikes - made for Remote Terrain. Unaplogetically addicted to nature. Quality mountainbikes since 1995!

At Northrace, we don’t just sell mountain bikes-

We build them! Since 1995, our independent bike manufactory has been dedicated to crafting high-quality mountain bikes and gear from the ground up. Every frame is handmade with precision, designed for serious riders and the toughest, most remote terrain. What makes us different? We're not a brand outsourcing production—we're a team of builders, riders, and nature-lovers who believe that the best bikes come from the workshop, not the warehouse.

We are strategists, designers and developers. Our bikes are engineered for the wild and built to last.
